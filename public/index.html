APRIL 18, 2022 — HIGHWAY 401, ONTARIO, CANADA
A family from Laval enjoying a trip to Toronto during the Easter break. The children visited the aquarium, watched a hockey game, laughed together. It was their last trip.

On the way back, on Highway 401, traffic slows down. Their car stops in traffic. Behind them, a 40-ton tractor-trailer approaches at 99 km/h. No one warned them. No one could.

The driver isn't watching the road. He's looking at his phone. The dashcam proves it. The data confirms it. He never braked.

In a split second, two children and their grandmother were killed. The mother survived. She had to bury her own children and her mother. Because a man was looking at his phone. Because no one had a system to stop him.

The truck driver received 5 years. The children never got a second chance.
